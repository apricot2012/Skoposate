Being a Translator

Join us in offering multilingual resources to the programmers community!

As a translator, as I interpreted lines after lines of text, I sometimes let my mind drift into the subject. As I dissected the meanings and tried to find the perfect expressions in another language, my understanding of the original context got better and better. Just like how tutoring improves your own comprehension of the subject, translating does the same. As a translator on Skoposate, you can build your experience and refine your own understandings on the subject at hand.

If you are new to the entire concept of localization, but have all the language skills, feel free to email us inquiries at skoposate@gmail.com and register using the form below.

If you are an experienced translator, please fill out the form and expect to hear from us soon!
